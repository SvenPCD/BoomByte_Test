----Controls----

A + D :Rotate Camera Around White Ball
Hold Space : Makes the hitstrength oscillate between max and min strength
Release Space : Shoot the White ball

Replay button replays your previous shot
Slow Replay button replays your previous shot at half speed
Skip Replay Skips the replay and returns you to your correct position.


----Goal----
You get 1 point each time you manage to hit the yellow and red ball in one shot.
Try and get 3 points in as little shots and time as possible.
